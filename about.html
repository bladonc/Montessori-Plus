About Us

Montessori Plus Preschool Academy (formerly Spirit West Preschool Academy) is a hands-on learning center with a low 6 - 1 student / teacher ratio to encourage and foster development.

Mrs. Susan Schuberthas received her B.Ed. and is a certified Child Development Supervisor. She has taught previously in the Parkland School Division for 6 years, and has taught Montessori preschool for over 25 years. She holds a grade 8 Royal Conservatory of Music Diploma.

Mrs. Lori McCrae has recieved her B.F.A. and B.Ed. She has also recieved her Child Development Supervisor certification. She has worked previously as an artist, book illustrator and art instructor. Lori has taught Montessori for over 20 years.
